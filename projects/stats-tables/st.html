Kalandra is a Norwegian band founded in 2018. They create alternative Nordic folk music that blends cinematic soundscapes, pop elements, and ethereal vocals. Many of their songs draw inspiration from Norse language and mythology, weaving atmospheric storytelling with emotional depth. The band was founded by lead vocalist and songwriter Katrine Stenbekk and guitarists Jogeir Daae Mæland and Florian Bernhard Döderlein Winter. Kalandra has also composed music for the video game Kingdom Two Crowns: Norse Lands and the HBO Nordic TV series Beforeigners.
