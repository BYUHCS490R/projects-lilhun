The Journey Begins...

Treat yourself to the stunning views along one of Ireland’s most famous scenic drives! The Ring of Kerry can be explored in a single day or spread out over several, depending on how many stops you make and how much time you want to spend exploring. It’s best experienced with your own car so you can reach the hidden places that tour buses — or walking — can’t easily access. Below are some recommended stops for your drive, but feel free to skip or add as you wish.

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 1: Muckross Abbey

A beautiful abbey with a yule tree rumored to be as old as the abbey itself!

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 2: Gap of Dunloe

A breathtaking drive with cliffs on either side!

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 3: Lord Brandon's cottage, Black Valley

A great place to stop and eat after passing through the Gap of Dunloe! (restrooms included)

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 4: Rossbeigh beach

A perfect place to get out of the car and sink your toes in the sand, and enjoy the sea breeze.

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 5: Oratory Pizza, Cahersiveen.

Stop for lunch in Cahersiveen at an old church converted into a pizza restaurant!

Source: Guide to Ring of Kerry Drive!

Stop 6: Kerry Cliffs

These cliffs are argued to have better scenery than the cliffs of Moher, and are less overrun by tourists.

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 7: Skellig Ring

A great coastal drive that goes towards the Skellig Islands.

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 8: Staigue Stone Fort

An Iron Age stone fortress that you can explore, and even walk inside!

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)

Stop 9: Kenmare

Grab a bite to eat, explore this beautiful town, and see one of the best stone circles on the Ring of Kerry!

Source: RING OF KERRY IRELAND - What To See If You ONLY Have 1 Day (Local's Tips!)
