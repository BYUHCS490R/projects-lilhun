Why I like it...

Irish music is an auditory tradition, which means that songs were learned by ear. It is all about taking a song and making it your own. Everyone has their own interpretaition and even the most well known songs have many variations.

Instruments

There are a lot of instruments used in celtic music. I play the violin (or fiddle), pennywhistle, and flute. Someday I would like to learn the bodhran (irish drum), bagpipe, and harp.
